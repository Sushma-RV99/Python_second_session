    About Department of Computer Science and Engineering

    The department of Computer Science & Engineering was established in the year 1983. It offers four years B.E. course with an intake of 240 students. VTU has recognized the department as a research center for carrying out research work leading to M.Sc. (Engg) by Research and Ph.D. degree. Department is accredited five times by NBA. The department has highly qualified faculty. Well Equipped labs serve the purpose of the curriculum prescribed and also facilitate to carry out innovative projects to challenge the fast-changing needs of the society.
